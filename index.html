Days Missed

Not a single day missed. MashaAllah.

🌙 The Quiet Sacrifices

Every morning before Fajr, while the world was still asleep, you were already in the kitchen. Preparing warm meals, making sure everything was ready before the adhan. Not once did you complain. Not once did you miss a day.


And every evening, no matter how tiring the fast was, you laid out beautiful iftars that turned our table into a blessing. The dates, the fruits, the dishes you made with so much love. Our home smelled like Ramadan because of you.

✨ What I See

I see a woman who gives without asking for anything in return. A woman who fasts all day, yet still has the energy to serve her family first. A woman whose patience is a quiet form of ibadah.


You don't just feed us. You nourish us with your love, your care, your dua. And that is something no words can fully capture.

Duas for You

تَقَبَّلَ اللَّهُ مِنَّا وَمِنْكُم
"May Allah accept from us and from you."

رَبَّنَا تَقَبَّلْ مِنَّا إِنَّكَ أَنْتَ السَّمِيعُ الْعَلِيم
"Our Lord, accept from us. Indeed, You are the All-Hearing, the All-Knowing." (Quran 2:127)

رَبَّنَا آتِنَا فِي الدُّنْيَا حَسَنَةً وَفِي الْآخِرَةِ حَسَنَةً وَقِنَا عَذَابَ النَّار
"Our Lord, give us good in this world and good in the Hereafter, and save us from the Fire." (Quran 2:201)

اللَّهُمَّ بَارِكْ لَهَا فِي حَيَاتِهَا وَصِحَّتِهَا وَعَمَلِهَا
"O Allah, bless her in her life, her health, and her endeavors."

اللَّهُمَّ اجْعَلْهَا مِنَ السُّعَدَاءِ فِي الدُّنْيَا وَالْآخِرَةِ
"O Allah, make her among the happy ones in this world and the Hereafter."

رَبَّنَا هَبْ لَنَا مِنْ أَزْوَاجِنَا وَذُرِّيَّاتِنَا قُرَّةَ أَعْيُنٍ وَاجْعَلْنَا لِلْمُتَّقِينَ إِمَامًا
"Our Lord, grant us from our spouses and offspring comfort to our eyes and make us leaders for the righteous." (Surah Al-Furqan, 25:74)
